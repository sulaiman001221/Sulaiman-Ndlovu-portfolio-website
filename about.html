My View on AI

I personally believe that AI won't take our jobs. Instead, it enhances our lives as software developers. AI makes us more productive, teaching us quickly and enabling us to solve problems that have already been addressed efficiently. Our role as software developers is to discover unsolved problems, create solutions, and then teach AI so that it can assist others. This collaboration with AI not only accelerates our work but also drives innovation, allowing us to focus on more complex and creative challenges.
